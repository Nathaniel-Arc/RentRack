Essential Safety Guidelines

Verified Student Accounts

All users must verify their identity with a valid WMSU student ID and email address.

  • Check the verification badge on user profiles
  • Report any suspicious accounts immediately
  • Never share your password with anyone

Campus Meeting Points

Always meet at designated safe zones during daylight hours and operating times.

  • Use well-lit, public areas on campus
  • Meet during peak hours when staff are present
  • Suggested locations: Library entrance, Student Center

Bring a Friend

We recommend bringing a fellow student for added safety during exchanges.

  • Let someone know where you're going
  • Share your meeting details with a friend

Document Everything

Keep records of all rental agreements and item conditions.

  • Take photos of items before and after rental
  • Save all messages and communication
  • Use the in-app messaging for all discussions

Rental Best Practices

Inspect Before Renting

Thoroughly check the item condition before accepting it

Clear Communication

Discuss all terms, conditions, and expectations upfront

Secure Your Belongings

Keep items safe and return them in the same condition

Report Issues

Contact support immediately if problems arise

Prohibited Items

The following items are not allowed on RentRack

Weapons or dangerous materials
Stolen or counterfeit goods
Items requiring special licenses
Illegal substances or paraphernalia
Personal identification documents
Hazardous chemicals or materials

Need Help or Have Concerns?

Our safety team is here to help. Contact us immediately if you experience any issues.
